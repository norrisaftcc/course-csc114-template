## Expectations and Grading

Because you as a student are able to select your path (and associated difficulty level), grading will vary based on the expectations the student sets for themself in the course. This is by design; as a pilot program, we do not yet know what's most effective to teach and learn this material, so trying several approaches and comparing results afterwards is our "experiment" to gather data. Since you're being used as a test subject, it's only fair to provide the benefit of the doubt. Thank You For Your Cooperation.
